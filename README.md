# Economy Bot
We want a rich economy so armies and conquest eventually hits a natural limit. Maintenence costs will become to high, businesses affect war efforts, and the need for money drives player actions. Also by allowing trading, investing, and other deep economic actions players that horde money have war potential without needing a standing army, and therefore we allow weighted political RP
